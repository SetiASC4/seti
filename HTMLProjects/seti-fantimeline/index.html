Kanye West

1997

Kanye Omari West was born on June 8th, 1977. He was born in Atlanta, Georgia. His parents were Ray West and Donda West.

1980

At the age of 3, West's parents divorced. He then moved to the south side of Chicago, Illinois, with his mother. He spent summers with his father.

1994

West graduated from Polaris High School in 1994. He decided to go to college afterwards. He still lived in Chicago at the time.

2000

West believed he was not learning fast enough. He dropped out of art school at Chicago State University in 2000. He later pursued his musical career.

2001

Kanye West eventually became known as a man who knew his music. He was hired by famous rapper Jay-Z to produce tracks for his new album, The Blueprint. The album sold over 420,000 copies in a week alone. West soon after produced for artists such as Ludacris and Beyoncé.

2004

On February 10, 2004, Kanye West's debut album The College Dropout was released under Roc-A-Fella records. It becamean instant classic peaking at #2 on the Billboard 200. It's been certified Platinum in America twice. West alsoproduced every song.

2005

Kanye West's long awaited sophomore album, Late Registration, was released on this date. It was almost as successful as The College Dropout. Over 3,000,000 copies have been sold in America.

2007

West's third studio album was released on September 11, 2007. It was his most successful release so far. The album was put against heavy competition with 50 Cent's album sales.

2008

November 24, 2008, marks the date of West's fourth album 808s & Heartbreak. It had more R&B and Pop influences than Hip Hop. It peaked at #1 on the Billboard 200.

2009

At the 2009 MTV Video Music Awards, Taylor Swift won the award for Best Female Video. During her acceptancespeech, Kanye West interrupted her saying "Beyoncé had one of the best videos of all time!" He got booed off the stage for this and many people were disgusted with him.

2010

Kanye West released My Beautiful Dark Twisted Fantasy on November 22, 2010. It is his most highly acclaimed albumso far. Metacritic, a site that averages critic ratings out of 100, calculated the album with a score of 94. It's the most hyped album of the decade.
