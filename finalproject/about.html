Our Mission

The ASH Foundation is a non-profit organization that helps rising sophomores and juniors understand what they would like to major in before they apply to colleges. Our mission is to have less students go into college undecided and have an idea of what they'd like to pursue as a major/career. We are hoping College students can gain college credit for joining our program.
