Curriculum

Mentee/Mentor Pairing

Rising sophomores and juniors will be able to apply for a major they would like to study/learn about. They will then apply and be paired up with an undergraduate student who is currently majoring in that major.

How ASH Works

When a high school student and a college student both input a common major, they will be paired together. After these two people are paired, they will video conference once a week. Students will meet up with their mentor atleast once a month.

What happens when you get in

You will be able to learn about any major you want by the time college comes around. After this program, you will gain a better understanding of whether you'd like to pursue a certain major or not. For the mentor, you will be able to gain college credit for helping out a mentee.
